## Project Overview

This project has been developed as part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. The objective of this project is to build awareness and practical understanding of digital tools, professional online presence, communication etiquette, and cyber safety.

The project is structured into five tasks, each focusing on a specific aspect of digital literacy and its real-world applications.

## Task Summary

### Task 1: Infographic Creation

A digital literacy awareness infographic was created using Canva. It highlights key topics such as digital tools, safe internet practices, and professional online behavior.

### Task 2: Digital Portfolio

Professional profiles were created and updated on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to establish a strong digital presence.

### Task 3: Coding and Collaboration Platforms

A beginner-level coding challenge was completed on HackerRank. Additionally, a Google Form quiz was created to assess digital literacy awareness, with responses linked to Google Sheets.

### Task 4: Email Etiquette

Two professional emails were drafted, and a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible digital communication was prepared.

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

A case study on cybercrime was developed along with a prevention checklist focusing on safe online practices, especially for financial transactions.

Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

•	Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, passwords, or bank details with anyone 
•	Do not click on unknown or suspicious links or messages 
•	Always verify the sender before making any online payment 
•	Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts 
•	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible 
•	Keep your apps, devices, and antivirus updated regularly 
•	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions 
•	Always check payment requests carefully before approving 
•	Log out from accounts when using shared devices 
•	Be cautious of calls claiming to be from banks or customer care 

UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
•	Never accept payment requests from unknown people 
•	Remember: to receive money, you do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN 
•	Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money 
•	Do not scan unknown QR codes 

Report Cybercrime:
